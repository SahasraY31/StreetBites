Welcome to Street Bites

What is Street Bites?

Street Bites is an online platform where users can find food trucks nearby based on their preference of food and location. Additionally, food truck owners can see where to park their trucks to optimally sell their food and see what the market value is like.about

How does it work?

Our goal is to successfully allow users and food truck owners to have an easier way to improve their quailty of life through navigation and preferences.

About Us

Our Goal

Our goal is to successfully allow users to search and view food trucks. Additionally, we value small businesses and many food trucks out there are from small businesses. This allows for growth and support of small businesses as well as improving the quality of life for all of our users.
